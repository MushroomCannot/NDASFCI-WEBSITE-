NOTRE DAME'S VISION MISSION STATEMENT

A God-centered community of friends, compassionate and dynamic, renowned for its academic excellence, pastoral involvement, option for the poor, and care for the environment under the guidance of Notre Dame our Mother.

We commit ourselves to pursue academic excellence, programs and activities, to protect and preserve the environment, to facilitate pastoral involvement towards the building and realization of a God-centered and Mary-like community.

The High School Department continued up to the present carrying the new name of Notre Dame Academy. Fr. Camomot was succeeded by Juan Boltron, then by Fr. Jose Motus, and then by Fr. Jorge Kintanar. The last Parish Priest Director of Notre Dame Academy was Fr. Constantino Batoctoy. He was responsible for the construction of the present high school building, with the support of the late Julio Cardinal Rosales, one of the proprietors of the school. In school year 1969-70, Cardinal Julio Rosales installed Sr. Lilia Hilarion, a ICM nun, as the first Directress and Principal of Notre Dame Academy.
